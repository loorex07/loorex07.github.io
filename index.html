APSL + ATHENA = ATFRL

Applied Termal Fluid Research Laboratory

Innovative research on propulsion, combustion, themal and energy system for mechanical and aerospace applications


Here, at Starkville, a beautiful gem in the State of Mississippi, we are dedicated to perform fundamental research to suggest better pathways for mitigating the global warming. We analyze fuel characteristics, conduct applied combustion and propulsion research, develop state-of-the-art thermal and energy system. Our interests of real-world application widely spread from ground vehicle propulsion, aero-propulsion to spacecraft propulsion system.
